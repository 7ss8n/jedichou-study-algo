* Rules of Big-O Notation
  * Coefficient rule: f(n) is O(g(n)) -> kf(n) is O(g(n))
  * Sum rule: f(n)+g(n) -> O(f(n)+g(n))
  * Product rule: f(n)p(n) -> O(h(n)p(n))
  * Transitive rule: f(n) -> O(g(n)), g(n)->O(h(n)) => Same
